HybridVLA focuses on the two mainstream paradigms of VLA models — diffusion-based and autoregression-based — and innovatively integrates diffusion and autoregressive action prediction within a shared-parameter LLM. It fully leverages the continuity and probabilistic nature of diffusion-based actions while preserving the reasoning capabilities of autoregressive modeling.
