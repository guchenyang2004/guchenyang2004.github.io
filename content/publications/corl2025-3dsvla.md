3DS-VLA enhances 3D spatial observation by introducing a 2D-to-3D position alignment mechanism, equipping pretrained VLM models with comprehensive 3D perception and enabling prediction of robust end-effector poses. To better understand the relationship between 3D observations and robot behavior, sequential 3D spatial constraints are introduced in imitation learning.
