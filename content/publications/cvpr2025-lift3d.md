Lift3D focuses on 3D visual perception for robotic manipulation, addressing challenges of 3D data scarcity and spatial information loss in tasks requiring geometric reasoning. It is the first to introduce both explicit and implicit 3D representations into 2D foundation models, designing a task-aware Masked Autoencoder to enhance depth reconstruction and proposing a 2D model lifting strategy to preserve spatial geometric structure in point cloud encoding.
