MLA employs an encoder-free multimodal alignment scheme to collaboratively perceive heterogeneous sensory modalities and predict future multi-sensory goals to facilitate physical world modeling. It directly interprets multimodal cues by aligning 2D images, 3D point clouds, and tactile tokens through positional correspondence.
