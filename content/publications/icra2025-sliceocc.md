SliceOcc proposes a vertical slice scene representation method for indoor environments. By leveraging attention mechanisms, it facilitates effective information exchange between planes and images as well as between adjacent planes, thereby improving the model's performance in complex indoor environments and achieving state-of-the-art performance among camera-based models.
