AC-DiT introduces a conditioning mechanism to guide the model in extracting base motion representations as contextual priors for predicting whole-body actions. It also designs a perception-aware multimodal conditioning strategy that dynamically adjusts fusion weights between various 2D visual images and 3D point clouds to meet the diverse multimodal perception needs across different stages of embodied mobile manipulation.
