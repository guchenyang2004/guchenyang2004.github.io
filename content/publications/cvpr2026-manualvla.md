ManualVLA is built on a Mixture-of-Transformers (MoT) architecture as a unified VLA framework that achieves coherent collaboration between multimodal manual generation and action execution. It endows VLA models with the ability to infer "how" processes from "what" outcomes, converting goal states into executable procedures.
